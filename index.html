How much does it cost?

Templates start at $5,000 JMD/month for hosting & maintenance and $50,000 JMD one-time setup. Custom projects quoted based on your needs. All hosting included.

How fast can I launch?

Template launches take 7–14 days. Custom builds take 3–6 weeks depending on complexity. We move fast.

Do I own the system?

Yes. You get full ownership of the code and can host it anywhere. We also offer ongoing hosting and maintenance if you prefer.

What support do you provide?

Training included with every launch. Ongoing support via WhatsApp. Priority support for Growth and Custom packages.

Can I customise a template?

Absolutely. The Template + Custom package adds 2–3 custom features. Or go Full Custom for anything you need.

Do I need to be tech-savvy?

No. We handle everything — build, deploy, and train. You just need to know your business. We handle the tech.
