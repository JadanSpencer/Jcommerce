How It Started

From One Gap
in the Market

Big J's started because of a problem every Jamaican driver knows too well — your car breaks down after hours, and there's nowhere to get the part until morning. You miss work. You lose money. You stress.

We built Big J's to solve that. One location, open until midnight, stocked with everything you need — from engine parts to tyres to batteries, for cars, trucks, buses, and bikes.

Since opening, we've become Kingston's go-to for late-night automotive emergencies. Taxi operators, delivery drivers, fleet managers, regular commuters — they all know: when everyone else is closed, Big J's is still here.
