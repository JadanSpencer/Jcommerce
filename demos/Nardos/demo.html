How it works

From scan to served —
in minutes.

A seamless experience for students and a no-fuss dashboard for Nardos. No app download. No account needed. Just scan and eat.

01
📷
Scan QR Code
Student scans the QR at Nardos or their dorm noticeboard. Opens instantly in their browser.
02
✍️
Enter Name & Hall
Quick onboarding — name, optional hall for delivery. Done in 10 seconds.
03
🍔
Browse & Order
Full menu by category. Add items, notes, group members. Place order instantly.
04
🔥
Kitchen Gets It
Order appears on Nardos' kitchen screen live. Timer is set. Student sees countdown.
05
✅
Collect or Receive
Student walks down at the right time, or delivery goes to their hall. Queue updates automatically.
What's included

Everything Nardos needs,
nothing it doesn't.

🍔
Full Menu System
Burgers, hot dogs, fries, snacks, cooked meals, beverages — all categories with prices, prep times, and item descriptions.
👥
Group Ordering
Multiple students, one order. Each person's items tracked separately. Perfect for dorm runs with friends.
🚶
Virtual Line
Students join the queue before leaving their room. They see their position live and leave at the right time.
⏱️
Live Countdown Timer
Nardos sets a timer when they start cooking. Students see a live countdown on their phones — no more "how long?" texts.
🛵
Hall Delivery
Students select delivery, pick their hall, pay J$200 extra. Nardos sees the delivery flag clearly on their kitchen screen.
📊
Kitchen Display
Live order cards for Nardos. Status flow: Pending → Preparing → Ready → Served. One tap to update. No paper, no confusion.
📝
Order Notes
Students can add notes per item or for the whole order. "No onions," "extra spicy," "leave by the door." Kitchen sees it all.
🔄
Real-Time Sync
Two phones open at once — student's status page and Nardos' kitchen screen update each other instantly. Demo it live.
